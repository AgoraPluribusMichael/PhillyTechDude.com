Consumer Protection Interstate Compact

We can work collectively with other states who want to protect consumers (I'm thinking California + whoever else), to create an interstate compact designed to act as a treaty to guide state government action. Together, we can cap credit card interchange fees and interest rates, protect the right to repair, eliminate the proliferation of microplastics and carcinogens, and reduce landfill waste.

Even if not all states participate, a coalition of populous blue states can force the corporations to play by the rules if they want to be competitive in American markets. Let's ban corporations from harming our society for profit.

21st Amendment Interstate Compact
